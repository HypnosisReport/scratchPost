Create A Sprite

Tutorials

Sprites are basically your little actors on the Scratch stage... but how do you create one? There's a few different methods.

Method 1: Library Method 2: Upload Method 3: Paint Method 4: Camera

Method 1: Library

1. There are lots of fun Sprites available in the Scratch library. Just click and choose.

2. Your chosen Sprite is now available to use.

Method 2: Upload

1. Maybe you'd prefer to use one of your own pictures or one you've saved from the internet. To do so, click and choose the file.

2. Your uploaded picture will appear in your project.

Method 3: Paint

1. If you want to really customise your Sprite, use the Scratch Paint pen. Click and play around with all the options.

2. Now your Sprite is one of a kind!

Method 4: Camera

1. Lastly, you can also take a picture straight from your webcam. Click and snap a photo.

2. Perfect. Take your best selfie and star in your own Scratch project.
